Success Stories

Read what some of our customers has to say after working with us.

Mr. Drajuruga Steven

A youth in Eria village, Moyo Subcounty, Moyo District, Westnile Region of Uganda, reported that; "I was a boda boda cyclist and the money I get is too small. When my child was hospitalized on referral , I had to sell off my boda (Motorcycle used for transportation of people & cargo), when I learnt about your radio extension program, the quality Advisory services you offered freely to the farmers and the agro inputs Apiviva Ltd brought closer to the community at a relatively subsidized prices inspired me to start vegetable farming as a business mainly specializing in Tomatoes and Water Melon and I was able to raise about Seven(7) Million from my Water Melon this season 2020. You have helped our people and God will reward you".

Mr. Ali (center) & Mr. Kasiime (left)

These are such rear Muslim youths who are engaged in vegetable farming as a business and had this to say; "We had valued Agriculture as the least important vocations to undertake, I Mr. Ali dropped out from my Bachelor's Degree in Education from Islamic University in Uganda (IUIU) Mbale because of lack of school fees but when I started practicing your advice on the mass media (radio) program, mobile call conversations and on the farm advices you offered, we were able to mobilize some money though we failed in some enterprise, the knowledge I acquired has inspired me, I'm sure from Tomatoes and Water Melon by 2021, I will be able to raise money to resume my Degree course in IUIU by 2022. Allah always blesses those who are generous with knowledge, He will reward you (Mr. Apiku Emmanuel, CEO Apiviva Ltd) for all that you have sacrificed to lift the farmers from poverty by educating them and providing good agro inputs".

When ever we called you on your mobile numbers, you responded with selfless Advisory services, your presence on the farm is rather more practical and workable, Apiviva Ltd has come to eradicate poverty in our communities, it's our first time to see agro stockist following up their farmers & give credits".
